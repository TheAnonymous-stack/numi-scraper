Grade 8 Mathematics - Volume of Rectangular Prisms

Question: 2_3 Skills: volume Type: Fill in the blank

Question:

What is the volume? ___ cubic millimetres

Rectangular Prism Visualization

Figure Description:

The image shows a rectangular prism with a length of 9 mm, a width of 5 mm, and a height of 4 mm.

Your Answer:

Enter your answer below:

cubic millimetres

Solution Steps:

  1. Step 1/3: Find the length, width, and height of the rectangular prism. length: 9 mm width: 5 mm height: 4 mm
  2. Step 2/3: Use these numbers in the formula. Volume = length \(\times\) width \(\times\) height = 9 \(\times\) 5 \(\times\) 4 = 180
  3. Step 3/3: The volume is 180 cubic millimetres.
