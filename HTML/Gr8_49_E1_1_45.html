What is the surface area? ___ square millimetres

Cube with side length: 9.0 millimetres
Click and drag to rotate the cube!

Solution Steps

1 Step 1: Each face of the cube is a square with sides that are 9 millimetres long.
2 Step 2: Find the area of one face: Area = side $\times$ side = 9 $\times$ 9 = 81 The area of each face is 81 square millimetres.
3 Step 3: There are 6 faces. Multiply: Surface area = 6 $\times$ 81 = 486 The surface area of the cube is 486 square millimetres.
Answer: 486 square millimetres
