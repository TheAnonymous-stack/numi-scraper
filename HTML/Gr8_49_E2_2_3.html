What is the surface area of this triangular pyramid? ___ square meters
3D Visualization

The image shows a triangular pyramid with a triangular base and three triangular faces. Each side of the base measures 9 cm. The height of the base triangle is labeled as 7.8 cm. Each of the three triangular faces has a height of 15 cm from the base to the apex of the pyramid.

Step-by-Step Solution
Step 1/3
Find the area of each face. $\frac{1}{2} \times 9 \times 15 = 67.5$ $\frac{1}{2} \times 9 \times 15 = 67.5$ $\frac{1}{2} \times 9 \times 15 = 67.5$ $\frac{1}{2} \times 9 \times 7.8 = 35.1$
Step 2/3
Add the areas of the 4 faces to find the surface area. 67.5+67.5+67.5+35.1=237.6
Step 3/3
The surface area of the triangular pyramid is 237.6 square centimetres.
Correct Answer:
237.6 square cm
