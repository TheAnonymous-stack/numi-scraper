Welcome to Pythagorean Theorem Practice

Master one of the most important theorems in mathematics! These interactive questions will help you understand and apply the Pythagorean theorem to find the length of the hypotenuse in right triangles.

Each question includes a visual triangle diagram, step-by-step solution, and clear explanations suitable for Grade 8 students.

The Pythagorean Theorem

$a^2 + b^2 = c^2$

Where a and b are the legs of a right triangle, and c is the hypotenuse.
