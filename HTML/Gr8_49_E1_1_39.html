Solution Steps

1 Step 1: Find the area of the faces in each face. $\frac{1}{2}\times6\times3=9$ $\frac{1}{2}\times6\times3=9$ $8\times5=40$ $8\times 5=40$ $6\times5=30$
2 Step 2: Add the areas of the 5 faces to find the surface area. Surface area$=9+9+40+40+30=128$
3 Step 3: The surface area is 128 square centimetres.
Answer: 128 square centimetres
